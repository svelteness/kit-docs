# KaTeX support

Mathematical expressions can be written inline, such as $a=1$, or as a block, such as the example below:

$$
b=2
$$ 

You can add all sorts of mathematical operations.

$$
\frac {a}{b} = \frac {1}{2}
$$

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{5}b*i = 30
$$


And also inline them $\sum_{i=1}^{5}b*i = 30$, just like that
